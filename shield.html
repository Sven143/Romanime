The Rising of the Shield Hero

(Tate no Yuusha no Nariagari)

SYNOPSIS:

Naofumi Iwatani, a lackluster Otaku who indulges in games and manga, is abruptly summoned to another realm. He learns that he is one of the four heroes chosen to wield legendary weapons and destined to rescue the world from its foretold doom. Despite being the weakest of the heroes and the Shield Hero, Naofumi soon realizes that not everything is as it appears. Betrayed and bankrupt, he is left to fend for himself with just his shield. Naofumi must transcend his limitations and acquire the status of the renowned Shield Hero to save the world.

Additional Information:
- The initial episode of the series premiered on December 27th, 2018, and the regular broadcast commenced on January 9th, 2019.
- The first episode had a duration of approximately 47 minutes, exceeding the typical 24 minute long episode.
