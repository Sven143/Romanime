Horimiya

(Hori-san and Miyamura-kun)

SYNOPSIS:

At first glance, it seems unlikely that Kyouko Hori and Izumi Miyamura would ever become acquaintances. Hori's striking appearance and academic excellence sharply contrast with Miyamura's reserved and distant demeanor. Nonetheless, as their paths intertwine, they expose a previously unknown side of themselves. Hori is overburdened with household obligations which leaves her with little time for socializing, while Miyamura conceals his body art under a subdued, rebellious persona at school. In spite of their contrasting personalities, Hori and Miyamura rapidly discover a common bond and form a strong connection. They frequently spend time together at Hori's place, revealing various aspects of themselves buried beneath public appearances.
